A little bit about me.
I'm a lifelong gamer with a passion for creation.

I started making games in paper sketchbooks in the first grade for my friends to play. Then I started modding real videogames. Eventually I studied computer science to take things to the next level and work on my own games.

​Inspired by great music and musicians, I learned to make my own compositions, play the piano, and produce music using a computer as my studio. I studied music composition, and have worked on music and sound for games.

My passion lies in creating something new and interesting. Videogames are an incredibly powerful medium and we are only beginning to scratch the surface of the possibilities.
