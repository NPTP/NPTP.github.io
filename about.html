About Me

I'm a lifelong gamer. I started making games on paper in the first grade. Soon after, I discovered game modding. Then I studied computer science to take things to the next level, and I continue to work on my own games.

​Inspired by what I heard in games, I learned to make my own compositions, play the piano, and expand the scope of music I enjoyed and created. I studied music composition, and have worked on music and sound for games, as well as my own projects.

My passion lies in creating something new and interesting. Videogames are an incredibly powerful medium and we are only beginning to scratch the surface of their possibilities. ​

Let's do it.
