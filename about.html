 I'm a lifelong gamer. I started making games on paper in the first grade. Soon after, I discovered game modding. Now, I'm studying computer science to take things to the next level.
​Inspired by great music in games and more, I learned to make my own compositions and play the piano. Eventually I studied music academically, and have worked on music and sound for games.
My passion lies in creating something new and interesting. Videogames are an incredibly powerful medium and we are only beginning to scratch the surface of its possibilities. ​
Let's do it.
