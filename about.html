I'm a lifelong gamer with a passion for creation.

I started making games in paper sketchbooks in the first grade for my friends to play. Soon after, I discovered game modding, and eventually studied computer science to take things to the next level. I continue to work on my own games.

​Inspired first by great music in games, then by great music from movies, and from bands and artists of all eras, I learned to make my own compositions, play the piano, and produce music using a computer as my studio. I studied composition, and have worked on music and sound for games. I continue to work on my own projects.

My passion lies in creating something new and interesting. Videogames are an incredibly powerful medium and we are only beginning to scratch the surface of their possibilities.
